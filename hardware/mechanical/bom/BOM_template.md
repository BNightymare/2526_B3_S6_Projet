## Categories

Group components by category:
- **Fasteners**: Screws, nuts, washers, bolts
- **Structural**: Frame, chassis, mounting plates
- **Bearings**: Ball bearings, bushings
- **Shafts**: Motor shafts, axles
- **Actuators**: Motors, servos, solenoids
- **Hardware**: Brackets, standoffs, spacers
- **Raw Materials**: Stock material to be machined
- **Custom Parts**: Parts to be manufactured

## Information to Include

For each item, specify:
1. **Quantity**: Total number needed
2. **Part Name**: Descriptive name
3. **Description**: Detailed specification
4. **Material**: Material type and grade
5. **Dimensions**: Critical dimensions
6. **Supplier**: Where to source
7. **Part Number**: Manufacturer/supplier part number
8. **Unit Cost**: Price per unit
9. **Notes**: Additional information

## Material Specifications

Common materials:
- **Aluminum**: 6061-T6, 7075-T6
- **Steel**: Stainless (304, 316), Carbon steel
- **Plastic**: ABS, PLA, PETG, Nylon, Acrylic
- **Brass**: For bushings and fittings
- **Copper**: For thermal applications

## Sourcing

Recommended suppliers:
- **Fasteners**: McMaster-Carr, Bossard, Würth
- **Hardware**: Misumi, RS Components, Farnell
- **Raw Materials**: OnlineMetals, Metal Supermarkets
- **Bearings**: SKF, NSK, Timken
- **Standard Parts**: Thorlabs, 80/20, MakerBeam

## Notes

- Keep BOM updated as design evolves
- Include lead times for long-lead items
- Note any critical or hard-to-source components
- Specify tolerances for custom machined parts
- Include alternative suppliers when possible
- Add weight information for each component
